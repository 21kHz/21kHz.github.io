WWOORRDDLLEE






You are trying to guess two five-letter words. A letter is green if it appears in the correct spot in either word. A letter is yellow if it appears in either word but in the correct spot in neither.

When you find one of the hidden words, it will have a * next to it. Warning: Just because a word is all green does not mean that it is one of the hidden words!

Click to start a new game with a random pair of words. Refreshing the page will return you to the daily puzzle.
