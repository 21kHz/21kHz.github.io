You are trying to guess two five-letter words. If a letter is green, it appears in the correct spot in either word. If a letter is yellow, it appears in either word but in the correct spot in neither.

When you find one of the hidden words, it will have a * next to it. Warning: Just because a word is all green does not mean that it is one of the hidden words!

Click to start a new game with a random pair of words. Click to restart the daily puzzle.
